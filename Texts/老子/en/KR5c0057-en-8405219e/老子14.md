![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az7]]
Reach for it: nothing to hold.

![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az8]]

![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az9]]

![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az10]]

![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az17]]

![[Texts/老子/老子14#^KR5c0057xtlsx014-1az20]]
