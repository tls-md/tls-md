   ![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az3]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az4]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az5]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az6]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az7]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az8]]
Get them straight before they get mixed up.

  ![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az9]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az10]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az11]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az12]]
from a heap of clay.

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az13]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az14]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az15]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az16]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az17]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az18]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az19]]
and not holding on,

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az20]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az21]]
(In all their undertakings,

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az22]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az24]]

![[Texts/老子/老子64#^KR5c0057xtlsx064-1az25]]
