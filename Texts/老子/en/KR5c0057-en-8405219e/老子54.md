 # ![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az3-h]]
Some rules {I follow Waley's interpretation of this chapter. It is Tao that plants and keeps; the various kinds of power belong to Tao; and finally in myself I see the Tao of self, and so on.}

   ![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az3]]
Well planted is not uprooted,

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az4]]
well kept is not lost.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az5]]
The offerings of the generations to the ancestors will not cease.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az6]]
To follow the way yourself

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az7]]
is real power.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az8]]
To follow it in the family

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az9]]
is abundant power.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az10]]
To follow it in the community

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az11]]
is steady power.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az12]]
To follow it in the whole country

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az13]]
is lasting power.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az14]]
To follow it in the world

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az15]]
is universal power.

  ![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az16]]
So in myself I see what self is,

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az17]]
in my household I see what family is,

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az18]]
in my town I see what community is,

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az19]]
in my nation I see what a country is,

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az20]]
in the world I see what is under heaven.

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az21]]
How do I know the world is so?

![[Texts/老子/老子54#^KR5c0057xtlsx054-1az22]]
By this.
