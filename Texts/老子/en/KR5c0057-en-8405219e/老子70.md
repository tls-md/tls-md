   ![[Texts/老子/老子70#^KR5c0057xtlsx070-1az3]]

![[Texts/老子/老子70#^KR5c0057xtlsx070-1az4]]
so easy to follow,

![[Texts/老子/老子70#^KR5c0057xtlsx070-1az5]]

![[Texts/老子/老子70#^KR5c0057xtlsx070-1az6]]

![[Texts/老子/老子70#^KR5c0057xtlsx070-1az13]]
