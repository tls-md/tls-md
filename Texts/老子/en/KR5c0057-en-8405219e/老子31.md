 # ![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az3-h]]
Against war

   ![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az3]]
Even the best weapon is an unhappy tool,

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az4]]
hateful to living things.

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az5]]
So the follower of the Way stays away from it.

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az6]]
Weapons are unhappy tools, not chosen by thoughtful people,

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az7]]


![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az8]]


![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az9]]


![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az10]]
to be used only when there is no choice,

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az11]]
and with a calm, still mind,

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az12]]
without enjoyment.

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az13]]
To enjoy using weapons

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az14]]
is to enjoy killing people,

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az15]]
and to enjoy killing people

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az16]]
is to lose your share in the common good.

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az22]]

![[Texts/老子/老子31#^KR5c0057xtlsx031-1az23]]
