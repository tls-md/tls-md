Techniques {Most of the scholars think this chapter is about meditation, its techniques and fulfillments. The language is profoundly mystical, the images are charged, rich in implications.The last verse turns up in nearly the same words in other chapters; there are several such “refrains” throughout the book, identical or similar lines repeated once or twice or three times.}

   ![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az3]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az5]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az6]]
Can you center your energy, be soft, tender,

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az7]]
and so learn to be a baby?

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az8]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az9]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az10]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az11]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az12]]

![[Texts/老子/老子10#^KR5c0057xtlsx010-1az13]]
