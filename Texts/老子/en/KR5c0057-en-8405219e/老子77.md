 # ![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az3-h]]
The bow

   ![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az3]]
The Way of heaven is like a bow bent to shoot:

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az4]]
its top end brought down,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az5]]
its lower end raised up.

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az6]]
takes from those who have,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az7]]
gives to those who have not.

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az8]]
Such is the Way of heaven,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az9]]
taking from people who have,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az10]]
giving to people who have not.

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az11]]
Not so the human way:

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az13]]
it takes from those who have not

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az14]]
to fill up those who have.

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az15]]
Who has enough

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az16]]
to fill up everybody?

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az17]]
Only those who have the Way.

  ![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az18]]
So the wise do without claiming,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az19]]
achieve without asserting,

![[Texts/老子/老子77#^KR5c0057xtlsx077-1az20]]
wishing not to show their worth.
