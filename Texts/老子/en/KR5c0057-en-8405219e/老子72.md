   ![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az3]]
When we don’t fear what we should fear

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az4]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az5]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az6]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az7]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az8]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az9]]

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az10]]
but don’t show themselves,

![[Texts/老子/老子72#^KR5c0057xtlsx072-1az11]]
