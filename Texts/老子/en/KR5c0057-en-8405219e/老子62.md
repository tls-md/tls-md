 # ![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az3-h]]
The gift of the way {I think the line of thought throughout the poem has to do with true reward as opposed to dishonorable gain, true giving as opposed to fake goods.}

   ![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az3]]
The way is the hearth and home of the ten thousand things.

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az4]]
Good souls treasure it,

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az5]]
lost souls find shelter in it.

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az6]]
Fine words are for sale,

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az7]]
fine deeds go cheap;

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az8]]
even worthless people

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az9]]
can get them.

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az10]]
So, at the coronation of the Son of Heaven

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az11]]
when the Three Ministers take office,

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az12]]
you might race out in a four-horse chariot to offer a jade screen;

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az13]]
but wouldn’t it be better to sit still and let the Way be your offering?

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az14]]
and let the Way be your offering?

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az15]]
Wasn’t it said: Seek, you’ll find it.

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az16]]
Hide, it will shelter you.

![[Texts/老子/老子62#^KR5c0057xtlsx062-1az17]]
So it was honored under heaven.
