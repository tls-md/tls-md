![[Texts/老子/老子8#^KR5c0057xtlsx008-1az4]]

![[Texts/老子/老子8#^KR5c0057xtlsx008-1az5]]

![[Texts/老子/老子8#^KR5c0057xtlsx008-1az6]]

![[Texts/老子/老子8#^KR5c0057xtlsx008-1az7]]
