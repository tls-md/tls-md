   ![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az3]]
Nothing in the world is as soft, as weak, as water;

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az4]]
nothing else can wear away the hard, the strong,

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az6]]

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az7]]

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az8]]
weak overcomes strong.

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az9]]

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az11]]

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az12]]
at the altar of earth;

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az13]]
you become a sacrificer

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az14]]

![[Texts/老子/老子78#^KR5c0057xtlsx078-1az15]]
