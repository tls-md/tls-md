- *gōng zǐ mù yí* [[TLS/Core/Syntactic functions/uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] **Prince Mùyí 公子目夷 (7th century) was a son by a concubine of Duke Huan of Song 宋桓公 (r. 681-651), and Duke Huan wanted to establish him as a successor. He was said to have declined the offer of the throne in 642.
