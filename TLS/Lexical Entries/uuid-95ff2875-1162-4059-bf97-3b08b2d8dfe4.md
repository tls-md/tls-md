# [[入]] *[[rù]]*  [[uuid-c20780b3-41f9-491b-bb61-a269c1c4b48f|vi]] [[BE AT HOME]]
be at home (with one's parents and family, as opposed to going away); stay at home LY 入則孝 "when at home he is filial"
 - [[春秋左傳]] **入**用其寵， / when you returned to you own state, you you made use of Qín's enthusiastic support;[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_005-237a.12)
 - [[孟子]] **入**以事其父兄， / being at home, they will thus serve their fathers and eldest brothers,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0001_tls_001-19a.10)
 - [[孟子]] **入**則孝， / He is obedient to his parents at home[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0001_tls_006-16a.10)
 - [[論語]] **入**則孝， / when with his family, will be filial[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0004_tls_001-6a.4)
 - [[論語]] **入**則事父兄， / in private one serves one's father and eldest brother.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0004_tls_009-17a.4)
 - [[呂氏春秋]] **入**則以輦， / when at home (on short distance travel) one uses a carriage,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3j0009_tls_001-14a.8)
