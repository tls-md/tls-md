# [[祖考]] *[[zǔ kǎo]]*  [[uuid-7ff85022-daa6-4ec8-892f-23641dce0f0f|NPpost-N]] [[ANCESTOR]]
LIJI: highest ancestors in the father's lineage; lineage of ancestors; sometimes also referring specifically to the father of the king
 - [[禮記]] 祭王父曰「皇**祖考**」， / 6. 13. In sacrificing to them, a grandfather is called 'the souvereign grandfather;'[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1d0052_tls_002-25a.17)
 - [[禮記]] 故至於祖、考之廟而后行。 / and therefore he is brought to the temple of his fathers, and then (the coffin) goes on its way.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1d0052_tls_004-18a.5)
 - [[禮記]] 曰**祖考**廟； / and the temple of his high ancestor.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1d0052_tls_024-5a.14)
 - [[禮記]] **祖考**廟， / and that of the (high) ancestor only,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1d0052_tls_024-5a.33)
