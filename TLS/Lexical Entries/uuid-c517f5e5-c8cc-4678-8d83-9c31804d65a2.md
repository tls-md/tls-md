# [[弘景]] *[[hóng jǐng]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[VINAYA MONKS]]
BUDDH: This monk lived from 634 to 712 and studied with the famous Vinaya-master Dàoxuán 道玄 (who is also the author of the XUGAOSENGZHUAN). At the same time he was a practicioner of the Tiāntái teaching (SGSZ, fasc. 5; YANAGIDA 1990: 396, fn. 269)
 - [[祖堂集]] 往荊州玉泉寺事**弘景**律師， / and went to the Yùqián monastery in Jīng province in order to attend to the Vinaya Master Hóngjǐng.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_003-1142a.42)
