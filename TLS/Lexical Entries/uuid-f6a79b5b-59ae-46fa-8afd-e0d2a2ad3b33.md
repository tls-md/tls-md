# [[樞]] *[[shū]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[GATE PART]]
door-"hinge" in the form of a beam round at the ends, which fits into what in modern Chinese is called ménjiù 臼 "hinge-holders" at the top and the bottom of the door frame.
 - [[呂氏春秋]] 戶**樞**不螻，動也。 / and the door hinge does not get infested with crawling insects, and this is because these things move.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3j0009_tls_003-14a.3)
 - [[莊子]] 桑以為**樞**；而甕牗二室， / jars with the bottoms out, hung with pieces of coarse cloth for protection from the weather, served as windows for its two rooms.6[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR5c0126_tls_028-14a.6)
