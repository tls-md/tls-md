# [[禳]] *[[ráng]]*  [[uuid-fbfb2371-2537-4a99-a876-41b15ec2463c|vtoN]] [[EXORCISE]]
to carry out deprecating sacrifices at times of natural disasters, often at times of floods, fires, appearances of stars, etc. In SJ occasionally sacrifice for good harvest.
 - [[史記]] 而君令一人**禳**之
                     / Yet my lord orders a single person to perform the jang sacrifice to it [the comet].[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR2a0001_tls_032-64a.29)
 - [[論衡]] 齊君欲**禳**彗星之凶， / The lord of Qi wanted to exorcise the evil associated with the broomstar,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3j0080_tls_017-3a.37)
