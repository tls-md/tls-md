# [[石]] *[[shí]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[WEIGHT MEASURES]]
one stone (measure of weight) equivalent to one 斛 "bushel" or ten 斗 "dippers" of rice etc. [anciently not necessarily dan4]
 - [[史記]] 重各千**石**， / These last weighed 1,000 piculs each[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR2a0001_tls_006-275a.10)
 - [[韓非子]] 斗**石**設而多益少。 / as long as bushels and stones are standardised, quantities will be levelled out.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_006-37a.5)
