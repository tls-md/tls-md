# [[鼓]] *[[gǔ]]*  [[uuid-fbfb2371-2537-4a99-a876-41b15ec2463c|vtoN]] [[DRUM]]
beat the drum to send (soldiers) into an attack
 - [[韓詩外傳]] 莊王援桴而**鼓**之， / King Chuang took a drumstick and beat with it.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1c0066_tls_006-18a.55)
 - [[韓詩外傳]] 援桴**鼓**之， / And seizing a drumstick he beat the signal for attack.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1c0066_tls_010-24a.42)
 - [[春秋左傳]] 公將**鼓**之。 / The duke was about to order the drums to beat an advance,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_003-114a.4)
 - [[春秋左傳]] 阻而**鼓**之， / Is it not proper for us[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_005-351a.15)
 - [[春秋左傳]] **鼓**而進之， / He then made the drums be beaten again, and urged his men on,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_007-82a.33)
 - [[春秋左傳]] 莒子親**鼓**之， / On this the viscount himself beat the drum,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_009-551a.25)
 - [[荀子]] **鼓**之而紂卒易鄉， / When they beat the signal to attack, the troops of Zhou Xin turned on their heels,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3a0002_tls_008-11a.42)

 - [[韓非子]] **鼓**之而士不起。 / When he beat the drums for the attack his officers did not rise.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_037-50a.5)
 - [[韓非子]] **鼓**之而士乘之， / Then he beat the drum for the attack and the officers stormed the wall.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_037-52a.14)
