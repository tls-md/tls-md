# [[繩床]] *[[shéng chuáng]]*  [[uuid-a8e89bab-49e1-4426-b230-0ec7887fd8b4|NP]] [[SEAT]]
BUDDH: this refers to an elevated platform in the meditation hall where monks sit and perform their meditation. The meditation platform usually streches at the two long sides of the meditation hall
 - [[祖堂集]] 師下**繩床**立。 / The master descended from the meditation platform and stood in attendance.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_006-2038a.31)

 - [[臨濟錄]] 師下**繩床**， / The Master got down from his corded chair.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0504a.69)
