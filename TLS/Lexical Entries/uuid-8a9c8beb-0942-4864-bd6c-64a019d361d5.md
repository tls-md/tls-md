# [[無事]] *[[wú shì]]*  [[uuid-091af450-64e0-4b82-98a2-84d0444b6d19|VPi]] [[uuid-f55cff2f-f0e3-4f08-a89c-5d08fcf3fe89|act]] [[INACTIVE]]
be without any business > being at leisure; have nothing to do in particular; do not engage in any specific activity in the Chan BUDDH context referring to the enlightened person who does not engage deliberately in any activity but rather perfectly responds to and mirrors his environment at any given moment; it is a key term in the teaching of the 臨濟錄
