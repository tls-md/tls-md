# [[公乘無正]] *[[gōng chéng wú zhèng]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[MALES OF HAN]]
Gōngchéng Wúzhèng 公乘無正(4th century). HF 22 describes him as a courtier in the state of Han 韓 and as a contemporary of >Zhang Qian 張譴 (4th century) and >Gongzi Shiwo 公子食我 (4th century). He is unattested in other sources.
