# [[簾]] *[[lián]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[CURTAIN]]
bamboo curtain, or curtain made of cloth, hung from the sides of a door or blocking the door preventing draft, and keeping insects out etc.
