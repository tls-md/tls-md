# [[兼金]] *[[jiān jīn]]*  [[uuid-ebc1516d-e718-4b5b-ba40-aa8f43bd0e86|NPm]] [[MONEY]]
according to 趙歧: gold of high quality which is twice as precious as the ordinary gold.
 - [[孟子]] 王餽**兼金**一百而不受； / His Majesty presented you with one hundred (yi)4 of double calibred gold, but you did not accept it.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0001_tls_004-14a.4)
