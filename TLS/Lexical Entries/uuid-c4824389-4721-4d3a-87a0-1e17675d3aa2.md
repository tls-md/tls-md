# [[麻谷]] *[[má gǔ]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[ZEN MONKS]]
BUDDH: His canonical name was Bǎochè 寶徹 and he was a dharma-heir of Mǎzǔ 馬祖. There is a short biographic entry in ZTJ 4.079,12; WU: 331; FO: 750 (see also JINGDECHUANDENGLU, fasc. 7). He resided on Mt. Mágǔ 麻谷in Púzhōu 蒲州, Shānxī 山西 province.

 - [[臨濟錄]] 時**麻谷**出問： / At that time Mayu came forward and asked:[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0496c.14)
 - [[臨濟錄]] **麻谷**拽師下座， / Mayu dragged the Master down from the lecture seat[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0496c.22)
 - [[臨濟錄]] 一手搊**麻谷**，云： / and with the other he seized hold of Mayu and said:[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0504a.71)
