# [[天龍]] *[[tiān lóng]]*  [[uuid-a8e89bab-49e1-4426-b230-0ec7887fd8b4|NP]] [[BUDDHIST DEITIES]]
BUDDH: heavenly beings and dragon spirits (demons) (which were often among the audience of the sermons of the Buddha; see 天龍八部)
 - [[妙法蓮華經]] 爾時比丘、比丘尼、優婆塞、優婆夷，及諸天、龍、鬼神等， / At this time the monks, nuns, laymen and laywomen,as well as the heavenly beings, dragons, spirits, and the others all had this thought:[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6d0001_T_001-0002c.1)
 - [[祖堂集]] 「當是**天龍**八部、釋梵助化之光。」 / "It was certainly the light of the eight kinds of heavenly beings and dragon kings, Indra and Brahma who assisst in teaching [the Buddha-dharma]."[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_005-2002a.1)
