# [[徐]] *[[xú]]*  [[uuid-bdf5c789-bfd8-4a3d-b6f7-2123f345d770|npr]] [[STATES]]
A state, older even than that of Zhōu, a powerful state at the early stage that was able to challenge the Zhōu, but finally destroyed in 512.
