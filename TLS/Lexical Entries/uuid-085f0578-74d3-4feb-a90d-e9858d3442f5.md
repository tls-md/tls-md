# [[三祇劫]] *[[sān qí jié]]*  [[uuid-db0698e7-db2f-4ee3-9a20-0c2b2e0cebf0|NPab]] [[uuid-2e7204ae-4771-435b-82ff-310068296b6d|buddhist]] [[PERIOD]]
BUDDH: three incalculable eons; three unimagineably long periods of time (referring to the period of time it takes for an unenlighened sentient being to gain the enlightenment of a Buddha; usually written 三阿僧祗劫 or 阿僧祗 ) SANSKRIT tri-kalpasaṃkheyeyaSee DDB: http://www.buddhism-dict.net/cgi-bin/xpr-ddb.pl?4e.xml+id('b4e09-963f-50e7-7947-52ab')
 - [[臨濟錄]] **三祇劫**空， / that this journey to enlightenment that takes three asamkhya kalpas to accomplish is meaningless.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0497c.36)
 - [[臨濟錄]] 於**三祇劫**中終歸生死， / then for three asamkhya kalpas you will remain in the realm of birth and death.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0500a.67)
