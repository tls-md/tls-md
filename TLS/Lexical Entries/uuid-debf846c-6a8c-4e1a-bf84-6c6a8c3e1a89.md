# [[崇侯]] *[[chóng hóu]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[RULERS OF SHANG]]
Marquis Hŭ of Chóng 崇侯虎 (11th century) was a trusted senior minister under Emperor Xin 帝辛, the King Zhou of Shang 商紂王. When the Earl Chang of the West 西伯昌 (future >King Wen of Zhou 周文王) showed his disapproval of the of Emperor Xin 帝辛 in the Marquis of Chong’s presence, the latter reports this loyally to Emperor Xin and warns against the future the Earl of the West. The Earl is imprisoned in Youli 羑里 (ZSJN 231). When the Earl of the West was liberated, he launched a punitive attack against Marquis Chong 崇侯. [LSCQ 2.4; SY 15.17 and 17.2; JINLOUZI 興王篇]
