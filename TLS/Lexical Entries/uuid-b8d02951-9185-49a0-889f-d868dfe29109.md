# [[罪福]] *[[zuì fú]]*  [[uuid-db0698e7-db2f-4ee3-9a20-0c2b2e0cebf0|NPab]] [[uuid-2e7204ae-4771-435b-82ff-310068296b6d|buddhist]] [[BUDDHIST PHRASES]]
BUDDH: guilt and good fortune (罪 refers to the results from keeping the precepts and performing good deeds, whereas 福 are the results from the performance of the ten evil deeds)
 - [[祖堂集]] **罪福**皆空無所住。 / Guilt and good fortune (merit) are both empty and without a anything to abide in.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1007a.13)
 - [[祖堂集]] **罪福**如幻起亦滅。 / Guilt and good fortune arise and perish like illusions.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1008a.17)
 - [[祖堂集]] 幻化之中無**罪福**。 / In illusionary transformations there is no guilt or good fortune.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1008a.53)
