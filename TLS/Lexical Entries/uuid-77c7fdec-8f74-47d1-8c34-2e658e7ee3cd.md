# [[天龍八部]] *[[tiān lóng bā bù]]*  [[uuid-a8e89bab-49e1-4426-b230-0ec7887fd8b4|NP]] [[uuid-5fae11b4-4f4e-441e-8dc7-4ddd74b68c2e|plural]] [[BUDDHIST DEITIES]]
BUDDH: the eight kinds of Buddhist deities and dragons/demon spirits (who are the protectors of the Buddha-dharma): tiān 天 (skr. deva), lóng 龍 (nāga), yèchā 夜叉 (yakṣa), āxiūluó 阿修羅 (asura), Jiālóuluó 迦樓羅 (garuḍa), gantapo 乾闥婆 (gandharva), 緊那羅 (kiṃnara), Móhouluójiā 摩　羅迦 (mahoraga)
 - [[祖堂集]] 「當是**天龍八部**、釋梵助化之光。」 / "It was certainly the light of the eight kinds of heavenly beings and dragon kings, Indra and Brahma who assisst in teaching [the Buddha-dharma]."[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_005-2002a.1)
