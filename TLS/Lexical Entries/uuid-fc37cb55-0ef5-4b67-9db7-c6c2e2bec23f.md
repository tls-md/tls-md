# [[棚頭]] *[[péng tóu]]*  [[uuid-a8e89bab-49e1-4426-b230-0ec7887fd8b4|NP]] [[ENTERTAINER]]
This word appeared during the Song dyn. and refers to persons engaged in all kinds of entertainment and especially gambling activities (e.g. rooster fights, bedding, etc.)
 - [[臨濟錄]] 云：「看取**棚頭**弄傀儡， / The Master said: "Look at the puppets performing on the stage.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0497a.56)
