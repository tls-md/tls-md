# [[近]] *[[jìn]]*  [[uuid-c20780b3-41f9-491b-bb61-a269c1c4b48f|vi]] [[NEAR]]
(spatial) be close, near; be a small distance

                     / Having heard and seen directly what is near him,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3a0002_tls_003-10a.4)
 - [[荀子]] 故**近**者歌謳而樂之，
                     / Hence 'those who are near him will sing his praises and rejoice in him,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3a0002_tls_008-4a.33)
 - [[荀子]] 故**近**者不隱其能，
                     / [HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3a0002_tls_009-15a.15)
 - [[孫子]] 遠而示之**近**， / when far away, we must make him believe we are near.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3b0003_tls_001-5a.7)
