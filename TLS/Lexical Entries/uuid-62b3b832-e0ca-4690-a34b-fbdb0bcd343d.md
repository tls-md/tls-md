# [[禪定]] *[[chán dìng]]*  [[uuid-db0698e7-db2f-4ee3-9a20-0c2b2e0cebf0|NPab]] [[uuid-2e7204ae-4771-435b-82ff-310068296b6d|buddhist]] [[MEDITATE]]
