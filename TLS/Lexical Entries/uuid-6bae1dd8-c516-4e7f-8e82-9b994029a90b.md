# [[斷見]] *[[duàn jiàn]]*  [[uuid-db0698e7-db2f-4ee3-9a20-0c2b2e0cebf0|NPab]] [[uuid-2e7204ae-4771-435b-82ff-310068296b6d|buddhist]] [[BELIEVE]]
BUDDH: view that there is a 'cutting off' (type of nihilistic worldview; heretical view that the self is destroyed at the time of death and that there is no rebirth, as such the deeds which one has performed during one's lifetime do not have any consequences); skr. uccheda-dṛṣṭi
 - [[臨濟錄]] 為順世間情，恐人生**斷見**， / were made to soothe worldly feelings. Lest people give way to nihilistic views,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0053_T_001-0500a.1)
