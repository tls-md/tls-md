# [[苦行]] *[[kǔ xíng]]*  [[uuid-091af450-64e0-4b82-98a2-84d0444b6d19|VPi]] [[uuid-f55cff2f-f0e3-4f08-a89c-5d08fcf3fe89|act]] [[CULTIVATE]]
BUDDH: to engage in ascetic practices, practice hardship (self-inflicted suffering in order to gain liberation; in the Buddhist context often used negatively since it can refer to excessive ascetic practices which do not conform to the 'Middle-way'); skr. dūkara-caryā; dhuta; vrata-tapa (see also toútuó 頭陀)
 - [[百喻經]] 見他頭陀**苦行**山林曠野塚間樹下， / They see a monk practising asceticism in the mountain forests, the wild open landscape, among the graves and under the trees.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6b0066_T_002-0548b.76)
 - [[祖堂集]] 「京城大德皆令布施、持戒、忍辱、**苦行**等求佛， / "The persons of great virtue of the capital all cause [us] to search for Buddha by giving alms, keeping the precepts, patience and practicing hardship (i.e. asceticism).[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_003-1132a.47)
