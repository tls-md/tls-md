# [[弔 / 弔吊]] *[[diào]]*  [[uuid-fbfb2371-2537-4a99-a876-41b15ec2463c|vtoN]] [[MOURNING]]
to bemoan the loss of (a person dear to one but not directly family-related to one), typically through a ritual visit of the bereaved family
 - [[說苑]] **弔**死問疾， / he offered condolences to the bereaved and paid visits to the sick,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=CH1a0907_CHANT_001-29a.34)
 - [[春秋左傳]] 使**弔**之。 / and sent an officer to present to her his condolences.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_009-552a.4)
 - [[莊子]] 向吾入而**弔**焉， / A little while ago, when I went in to mourn,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR5c0126_tls_003-5a.11)
 - [[莊子]] 老聃死，秦失**弔**之， / When Lao Tan6 died, Chin Shih went to mourn for him;[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR5c0126_tls_003-5a.2)
 - [[莊子]] 諸侯**弔**之，三年， / where the feudal lords went to console him for three years.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR5c0126_tls_026-12a.22)
