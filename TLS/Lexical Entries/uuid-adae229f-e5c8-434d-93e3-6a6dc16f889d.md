# [[車匿]] *[[chē nì]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[uuid-2e7204ae-4771-435b-82ff-310068296b6d|buddhist]] [[DISCIPLES OF BUDDHA]]
(BUDDH:) one of the servants in the palace of King Jìngfàn 淨飯 and the horse-keeper of Buddha; he later became one of the six initial disciples of Buddha; skr. Chandaka, pali Channa
 - [[祖堂集]] 潛命**車匿**鞁犍陟來， / He secretly ordered Chandaka to get ready (i.e. saddle) the horse Kanthaka.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1020a.2)
 - [[祖堂集]] 并脫頭冠、白馬等，付與**車匿** / and also took off his crown and handed them, together with the white horse and the other personal belongings over to Chandaka.259[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1020a.24)
