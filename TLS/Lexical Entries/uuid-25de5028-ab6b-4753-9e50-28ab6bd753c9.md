# [[段規]] *[[duàn guī]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[MALES OF HAN]]
Duàn Guī 段規 (fl. 453) was a ministerial adviser in Han 韓 to Han Kangzi 韓康子 and was instrumental in the murder of the Earl of Zhi 智伯 in 453. In ZGC Zhao 1 Duan Gui advises the ruler of the state of Han against acceding to demands for land by the Earl of Bo. According to GY Jinyu 9 and SY 5.27 Duan Gui is instrumental in the murder of the Earl.
 - [[國語]] 智襄子戲韓康子而侮**段規**。
