# [[歌]] *[[gē]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[uuid-7bbb1c42-06ca-4f3b-81e5-682c75fe8eaa|object]] [[SONG]]
song; popular song (also that of primitive peoples etc) 人聲曰歌. 歌柯也. 所歌之言是其質也. 以聲吟詠有上下如草木之有柯葉也. 故兗冀言 歌聲如柯也 Songs of humans are called gē. gē “song” is kē “branch.” The words sung, these are the substance. When using the voice one chants, the voice goes up and down, like plants and trees having stems and leaves. Thus in the Yuan and in the Jì areas they pronounce ky “sing” with a sound like kē “branch”.
