# [[佛肸]] *[[fó fú xī]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[MALES OF JIN]]
Bì Xī 佛肸 was a domestic officer of the Fàn 范 clan in Jìn, who had been put in charge of the town of Zhōngmóu 中牟 (loc. near Xīngtái and Hándān, Héběi Provence). This Zhōngmóu was in Jìn territory. Another town of the same name was located in Zhèng.
