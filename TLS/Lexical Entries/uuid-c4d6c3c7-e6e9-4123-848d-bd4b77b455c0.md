# [[徐]] *[[xú]]*  [[uuid-c20780b3-41f9-491b-bb61-a269c1c4b48f|vi]] [[SLOW]]
be slow and comfortable; be mild
 - [[春秋左傳]] 請行以觀王怒之疾**徐**， / and ask him to go and observe whether the king's anger be furious or slow,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_010-145a.14)
 - [[春秋左傳]] 視下，言**徐**。 / His looks were bent downwards, and his words came slow and low.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1e0001_tls_010-285a.3)
 - [[管子]] 故賢者安**徐**正靜， / Therefore the worthy ruler, calm and unhurried, remains perfectly quiescent.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0001_tls_015-12a.2)
 - [[莊子]] 其行身也，**徐**而不費， / In his movement he was easygoing and did not wear himself out.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR5c0126_tls_033-14a.38)
