# [[長老]] *[[zhǎng lǎo]]*  [[uuid-a8e89bab-49e1-4426-b230-0ec7887fd8b4|NP]] [[MONK]]
BUDDH: in the Buddhist context originally referring to the major students of Buddha, more generally used as honorary appellation for monks: eminent monk
 - [[祖堂集]] 「莫便是**長老**家風也無？」 / "Is that not just the (teaching) method of eminent monks (or: disciple of the Buddha)?"[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_016-4118a.39)
