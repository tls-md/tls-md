# [[東郭牙]] *[[dōng guō yá]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[MALES OF QI]]
Dōngguō Yá 東郭牙 (seventh cent.) was a contemporary of >Duke Huan of Qi 齊桓公. He is described as an aide and an able remonstrator in HF 33. He is listed together with >Xi Peng 隰朋, >Xian Ning 弦甯, >Ning Ji 甯疾, >Prince Cheng-fu 王子成甫, and the famous >Guan Zhong 管仲 (d. 643) as an able political adviser.
