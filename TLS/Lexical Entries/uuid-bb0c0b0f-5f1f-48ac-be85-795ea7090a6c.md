# [[毫]] *[[háo]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[HAIR]]
fine long hair, primarily on animals, but by extension also used to refer to human hair
 - [[西京雜記]] 毛皆以秋兔**毫**毫 / and the bristles were all from the hair of autumn hares[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=CH1a0936_CHANT_001-3a.8)
 - [[孟子]] 明足以察秋**毫**之末， / My keen eyesight is sufficient to see the tip of an autumn hair,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0001_tls_001-42a.6)
 - [[孟子]] 思以一豪挫於人， / In his reflection, he believed, that if his single hair has been bent by somebody,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0001_tls_003-12a.7)
 - [[韓非子]] 若秋**毫**之末。 / as insignificant as the tip of an autumn hair.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_014-68a.9)
 - [[祖堂集]] 如一**毫**置之太虛； / they are like a tiny hair placed in the great void (open space, nothingness)[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_005-2032a.47)
