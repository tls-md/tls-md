# [[竟]] *[[jìng]]*  [[uuid-a2deab25-a677-4576-bd3d-f0025e0101f9|vpostS1.adS2]] [[WHEN]]
This is a rather grammaticalized usage of 竟 marking the temporal anteriority of S1 to S2: after S1, S2 (compare the more current 了 and 已)
 - [[賢愚經]] 一切臥**竟**， / 你自己伺候東家所有的人睡下之後，[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6b0059_T_005-0384a.79)
 - [[賢愚經]] 作禮繞**竟**，求索入道。 / 行禮並繞行一圈以後，要求出家，進入佛道[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6b0059_T_005-0384c.51)
 - [[百喻經]] 取藥塗毒**竟**， / Once you have taken the medicine and you have applied the strong substance,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6b0066_T_004-0557c.50)
 - [[祖堂集]] 出家**竟**， / after [Gānlùfàn] had renounced home,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1016a.48)
 - [[祖堂集]] 爾時，釋迦如來成道**竟**， / At that time, after Śākya Tathāgata had attained the Way (i.e. was enlightenment)[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR6q0002_Yan_001-1023a.1)
