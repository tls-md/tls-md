# [[戴驩]] *[[dài huān]]*  [[uuid-c43c0bab-2810-42a4-a6be-e4641d9b6632|NPpr]] [[ARISTOCRATS OF SONG]]
Dài Huān 戴驩 (fl. ca. 370) was a Senior Stewart 大宰 in Song 宋 and must have been a contemporary of Huang Xi 皇喜 . He is unattested outside HF. [HF 30.44; 30.46]
