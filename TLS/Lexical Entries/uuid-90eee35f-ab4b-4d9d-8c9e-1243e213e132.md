# [[輗]] *[[ní]]*  [[uuid-8717712d-14a4-4ae2-be7a-6e18e61d929b|n]] [[CARRIAGE POLE]]
LY: Small piece of wood inserted in two yuán 轅 to link the héng 衡 into the two yuán 轅 "carriage poles", the single carriage pole being known as the zhōu 輈 "carriage pole".
 - [[論語]] 大車無**輗**， / If a large vehicle doesn't have its large cross-bar at the end of the carriage pole[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR1h0004_tls_002-23a.5)
 - [[韓非子]] 「吾不如為車**輗**者巧也。 / "I am not as clever as those people who make ní-bars for the carriage.[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_032-24a.9)
 - [[韓非子]] 巧為**輗**， / He regards it as skillful to make ní-crossbars across the shaft,[HXWD](https://hxwd.org/textview.html?location=KR3c0005_tls_032-26a.4)
