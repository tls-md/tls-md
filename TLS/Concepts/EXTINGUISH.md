# Definition
CAUSE TO STOP BURNING.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for extinguishing a fire is xí [[息]].
2. Miè [[滅]] "destroy" is occasionally used to refer to the extinguishing of fires.
