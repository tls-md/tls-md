# Definition
JOINT BETWEEN TRUNK AND ARM.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for the shoulder is jiān [[肩]].
NB: Bàng [[膀]] and jiǎ [[胛]] are post-Han words for the shoulder.
