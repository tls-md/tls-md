# Definition
SHOUT so as to PRODUCE an INTENSELY LOUD ANIMAL NOISE.
# Criteria and general notes
## Modern Chinese Criteria
咆哮 is the general word for the roar of an animal.
怒吼 focusses on the roar as a sign of anger.
