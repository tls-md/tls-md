# Definition
FATHER of one's HUSBAND OR WIFE.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for a father-in-law is jiù 舅.
2. Zhàng rén 丈人 is a colloquial term for a father-in-law.
