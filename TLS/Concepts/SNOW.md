# Definition
WHITE FROZEN RAIN.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The dominant word is xuě 雪.
2. Xiàn 霰 are snowflakes, and the structure of these drew early Chinese attention.
3. Bīng 冰 is ice. See ICE.
