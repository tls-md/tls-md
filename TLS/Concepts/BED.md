# Definition
FURNITURE ON which one SLEEPS.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current standard word for a bed is chuáng [[床]].
2. Kuāng [[筐]] refers to (bamboo-made??) bedding used on a bed.
