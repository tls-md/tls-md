# Definition
ROOM USED FOR PRODUCING MEALS.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current word for the kitchen is páo [[庖]]／[[炮]].
2. Chú [[廚]] is said to be a colloquial expression for a kitchen.
