# Definition
OFFICIAL who is SENT by the GOVERNMENT to ACT:perform a TASK.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current formal word for an envoy is shǐ zhě 使者.
2. Shǐ/shì 使 can refer to an envoy.
