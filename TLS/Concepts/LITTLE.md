# Definition
SMALL in QUANTITY of a SUBSTANCE.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for a small quantity of some stuff is guǎ [[寡]].
2. Shǎo [[少]] can also be used to a small quantity of stuff.
