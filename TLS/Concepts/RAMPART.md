# Definition
TOP of CITY WALL.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. Dié 堞 refers both to the battlements on the wall and to the crenels of it.
2. Pì 埤 is another term for the battlements on the wall.
Cf also Nǔqiáng 女牆
