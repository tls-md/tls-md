# Definition

# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria
1. Xiù 臭／嗅 "try to smell" relates to wén 聞 "perceive the smell of" exactly like shì 視 "look at" to jiàn 見 "see".
NB: Compare 嘗 "try to taste". See TASTE.
