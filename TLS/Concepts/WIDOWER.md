# Definition
HUSBAND BEREAVED of his WIFE OR OLD MAN LACKING a WIFE.
# Criteria and general notes

HUMAN AFFAIRS UNMARRIED MALE 26.
鰥，獨身未娶的成年男性的通稱，特指老而無妻者。
曠夫，無妻的獨身男子。
獨夫，無妻的男子，少用。
