# Definition
The FRUIT or seed of the oak-tree; an oval nut growing in a shallow woody cup or cupule.
# Criteria and general notes
## Old Chinese Criteria

[DIALECT/STANDARD]

[STANDARD]
2. Xiàng [[橡]] is probably a dialect word, and can refer to acorns.
